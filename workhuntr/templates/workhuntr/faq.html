Frequently Asked Questions

What is Workhuntr?

WorkHuntr is a website that aids users in their job search or their search for help on projects. Each user is divided into one of two categories: a huntee, someone who is looking for assistance on a job/project, or a hunter, someone who is looking to be hired for a job/project.

How easy is it to start using Workhuntr?

Workhuntr is as easy to use as any other common website. Simply create an account and begin connecting to other users!

Is Workhuntr safe and secure?

We strive to ensure that all users feel as safe as possible when using our website. We allow you to choose what your profile displays and whether your profile is public, we always encrypt your private information, and we always ensure that we only use what is absolutely necessary for getting you connected to potential employers or employees is only the start of the precautions we are taking to ensure safety and security.
